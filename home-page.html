TIL that honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.

Scientists explain that honey's unique chemical composition - low moisture content and high acidity - creates an environment where bacteria and other microorganisms cannot survive, making it one of nature's only foods that never spoils.
